Born in 1989, Chen received her BFA in Painting with an emphasis in Ceramics, from the Cleveland Institute of Art in Cleveland, OH, in 2016. She has previously received her BA in Philosophy in 2012, from the National Taiwan University in Taipei, Taiwan. She won the Grand Prize in 70th Annual Student Independent Exhibition, at Cleveland Institute of Art. She’s also a recipient of Joseph McCullough ‘48 Scholarship for Excellence in Painting and Ted Frost ’88 Scholarship for Travel. Her paintings have been included in the collections of the Cleveland Institute of Art, The Dealer Tire LLC, Baker Hostetler Collection, Southwest General Health Center, and University Hospitals Harrington Heart & Vascular Institute. Chen currently lives and works in Cambridge, MA.
